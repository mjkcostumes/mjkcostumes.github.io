RENTAL INQUIRY

MATTHEW KILGORE launched MJK COSTUMES in 2019 to cater to theaters producing A CHORUS LINE. As a former dancer, he has a unique perspective of the fit and style of dancewear. WIth an intimate knowledge of A CHORUS LINE (from playing Mike, Larry, Tom and Roy while covering Mark, Larry, and Bobby), Matthew strives to honor and carry on the legacy of Theoni V. Aldredge's original design in his rental packages. From Cassie's classic red leotard and skirt to Paul's orange hoodie - no detail has been overlooked. 

Matthew is a member of IATSE Local 764 and currently works as the Tailor for The Drew Barrymore Show. His wardrobe credits include: THE TODAY SHOW (NBC), CBS MORNINGS (CBS), THE GOOD DISH (CBS), SPINNING GOLD, TINA (Broadway), HAMILTON (Broadway), FROZEN (Broadway), THE ICEMAN COMETH (Broadway), and MY FAIR LADY (Broadway). He has designed costumes for THE DREW BARRYMORE SHOW, BROADWAY BACKWARDS 2016, EASTER BONNET, BROADWAY BARES, and FIRE ISLAND DANCE FESTIVAL all for Broadway Cares/Equity Fights AIDS. Matthew has created costumes for FORBIDDEN BROADWAY, THE TODAY SHOW, and NORWEGIAN CRUISE LINES.

Matthew holds a Bachelor of Science in the Arts: Performing Arts from SUNY Empire State. He is a two-time
Emmy Award winning Costume Designer.
